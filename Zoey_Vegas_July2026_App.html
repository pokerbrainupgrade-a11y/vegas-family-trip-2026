Adventure Stop 1
Mermaid Lagoon
~ Pool day with real waves ~

Welcome to Mandalay Beach — your very own ocean in the middle of the desert! There's a wave pool with real waves, a lazy river that goes all the way around, and a splash zone just for you.

And then there's Shark Reef Aquarium — where you walk through a glass tunnel with sharks, sea turtles, and jellyfish swimming all around you. It's like being inside the ocean!

Click your fins together three times — you're a real mermaid now! 🧜‍♀️
🌊 Mandalay Beach

Included with hotel stay · Bring your floaties!

Adventure Stop 2 + 3
Carnival Castle & Magic Bubble
~ Rides, Oz, and fireworks! ~

First stop: Adventuredome at Circus Circus — the biggest indoor theme park ever built! Roller coasters, bumper cars, carnival games, and a free clown show. You can ride everything!

Then: The Sphere — a giant glowing ball building with the world's biggest screen inside. You're going to see the Wizard of Oz like you've never seen it before. Dorothy would be jealous!

You're in a real carnival castle, princess — and you're the queen of all the rides! 🎢
🎢 Adventuredome
Circus Circus · Indoor Theme Park
Roller coasters, bumper cars, carnival games, clown shows. The whole thing is inside a giant pink dome!
Tickets required · Free clown show at noon & 1pm
🌐 The Sphere
Wizard of Oz Experience
The world's biggest LED screen — it wraps all the way around you! Wizard of Oz has never looked this magical.
Tickets required · Mind-blowing experience

~ Princess Protocol ~
The 7 Princess Rules
Every queen needs her code!

Being a Vegas Princess is a big responsibility. Here are the 7 rules every diva must follow on this magical adventure!

1
Stay with the crew. A princess never wanders off alone — your crew is your royal guard!
2
Try one new food every single day. Brave princesses taste everything!
3
Use your inside voice in the hotel. Other guests are sleeping — even if you're too excited to sleep!
4
Sunscreen every morning. Even princesses need to protect their royal skin!
5
Say thank you to every person who helps you. Kindness is the most magical superpower.
6
Take mental photos of your favorite moments. Some memories are too magical for cameras!
7
Be the most sparkly version of yourself every single day. Vegas was made for you, Zoey!
